Our Story

About AutoForge Parts

Founded in 2009 in Chennai, AutoForge Parts started with a single mission: give every vehicle owner access to genuine parts at fair prices, backed by honest advice.

Today we operate one of South India's largest spare parts showrooms — over 12,000 SKUs spanning all major categories. We serve individual vehicle owners, neighbourhood garages, and large automotive workshops, with the same intensity regardless of order size.

Our team of certified mechanics and parts specialists brings decades of hands-on experience to every customer interaction. When you come to us, you get the right part the first time.

2009
Opened first store on Anna Salai — 200 sq ft, 500 part numbers, zero compromise on quality.
2013
Expanded to current 5,000 sq ft showroom. Crossed 5,000 active SKUs.
2018
